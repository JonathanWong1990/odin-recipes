The Shin Ramen

To prepare a delicious bowl of Shin Ramen, start by boiling 550ml (about 2 1/3 cups) of water in a pot. Once boiling, add the noodles and the soup base from the Shin Ramen packet. Stir occasionally and let the noodles cook for about 4-5 minutes until they reach your preferred level of tenderness. For an enhanced flavor, you can also add a few slices of your favorite vegetables like mushrooms, carrots, or spinach during the last minute of cooking.

While the noodles are cooking, you can prepare some optional toppings to make your ramen even more satisfying. Fry an egg sunny-side-up or poach it directly in the simmering soup for a protein boost. You can also add slices of cooked meat such as beef, pork, or chicken, and garnish with chopped green onions, sesame seeds, or a drizzle of sesame oil. Once the noodles are cooked, transfer everything to a bowl and enjoy your spicy, flavorful Shin Ramen!

Ingredients

    Main Ingredients

  • 1 package of Shin Ramen
    • Includes noodles and soup base
  • 550ml (2 1/3 cups) water

    • Optional Ingredients

  • Vegetables
    • Mushrooms, sliced
    • Carrots, julienned
    • Spinach leaves
  • Protein
    • 1 egg (fried sunny-side-up or poached)
    • Cooked meat slices (beef, pork, or chicken)
  • Toppings
    • Chopped green onions
    • Sesame seeds
    • Sesame oil

Follow These Steps

  1. Boil Water: Bring 550ml (2 1/3 cups) of water to a boil in a medium-sized pot.
  2. Cook Noodles: Add the noodles and the soup base from the Shin Ramen packet to the boiling water. Stir occasionally and let the noodles cook for about 4-5 minutes until they reach your desired level of tenderness.
  3. Add Vegetables (Optional): If using vegetables like mushrooms, carrots, or spinach, add them to the pot during the last minute of cooking to soften them slightly.
  4. Prepare Optional Toppings: While the noodles are cooking, you can prepare additional toppings. Fry an egg sunny-side-up, or poach it directly in the simmering soup. Cook slices of meat like beef, pork, or chicken if desired.
  5. Assemble and Serve: Once the noodles and optional add-ins are ready, transfer everything to a bowl. Top with your prepared egg and meat slices, then garnish with chopped green onions, sesame seeds, and a drizzle of sesame oil. Enjoy your flavorful Shin Ramen!
