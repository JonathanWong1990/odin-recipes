Chicken Noodle Soup: A Comforting Classic

Chicken noodle soup is a timeless remedy for cold days and unsettled stomachs, known for its soothing broth, tender chicken, and hearty vegetables. This beloved dish combines the rich flavors of a homemade chicken stock with the simplicity of fresh vegetables and egg noodles. The result is a warm, nourishing soup that provides both comfort and sustenance. Whether enjoyed as a remedy for the common cold or simply as a wholesome meal, chicken noodle soup holds a special place in many hearts, evoking memories of home and family with every spoonful.

What sets chicken noodle soup apart is its balance of flavors and textures. The broth, often simmered with aromatic herbs and spices, serves as the perfect base for chunks of succulent chicken and a medley of vegetables like carrots, celery, and onions. The addition of egg noodles adds a satisfying chewiness that complements the tender chicken and crisp vegetables. This versatile soup is not only easy to prepare but also customizable to suit individual tastes, making it a staple in kitchens around the world. Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 3 large carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded or diced
  • 1 cup egg noodles
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Fresh parsley or dill for garnish

Follow These Steps

  1. Saut√© Vegetables: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion, sliced carrots, and celery. Saut√© for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables begin to soften. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  2. Add Broth and Seasonings: Pour the chicken broth into the pot and bring it to a boil. Add the dried thyme and dried parsley, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Reduce the heat and let the soup simmer for about 15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  3. Cook Noodles: Add the egg noodles to the simmering soup and cook according to the package instructions, usually about 6-8 minutes, until they are tender but still firm to the bite.
  4. Add Chicken: Stir in the cooked chicken and let it heat through for about 5 minutes. If using, adjust seasoning with more salt and pepper as needed.
  5. Serve: Ladle the soup into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley or dill if desired. Serve hot, enjoying the comforting and nourishing qualities of this classic chicken noodle soup.
